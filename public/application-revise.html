2.2 Course Outline and/or Training Agenda

What to Include

Your application package must include a course outline and/or training agenda that specifies:

  • Course title, course length, prerequisites (if applicable), and course fee
  • How the course will be delivered (online, in person, or blended)
  • A description of the topics covered and activities done during the course or training

PDF format, maximum 10MB

2.3 Administration Document

What to Include

Your administration documents must provide information about:

  • Instructor qualifications: The qualifications and responsibilities of the person or people conducting the training (and conducting the practical skills test for Level 2)
  • Training records maintenance: Your maintenance plan for training records (i.e., how you will maintain participant training records and ensure their accuracy)
  • Quality monitoring and evaluation: Your monitoring and evaluation plan for maintaining the quality of training and testing (typically in the form of policy, procedures, and related reports, including a mechanism to collect participant feedback)
  • Complaints and appeals: Your policy and procedures related to complaints and appeals
  • Registration, withdrawal, and refunds: Your policy and procedures for registration, withdrawal, and refund processes

PDF format, maximum 10MB
